About Me

Hello my name is Jake Harrison, I was born in Trenton Ontario on September 25th, 2005. I went to St. Paul's Catholic Secondary School for my highschool education, and during this time I ignited my passion for media design. I took 6 different art programs varying from Media Design, Communications Tech, and Photography.

Some of my other personal interests include music, photography, and the outdoors. My greatest passion and interest is music, although I do not know how to make music, I love making playlists to express my emotions. I also love the outdoors as I worked at a Provincial park over the summer.
